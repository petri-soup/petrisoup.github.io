welcome to my homepage!

In my work, I focus on evolved technological and biological symbiosis; In biology, symbiosis not only marks the origin of all life but demonstrates that a communal ecology outperforms solidary entities.
If the first stone tools were extensions of the body, then computational devices are extensions of the mind. Human-technology symbiosis expands our ability to exist in imagined worlds where we see reality in a refracted light.
In my paintings, renders, and code, creatures and their connected mythologies populate biomes where each organism relies on the next, like modules of a vast circuit.
